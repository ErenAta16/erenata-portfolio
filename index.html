P/03

Gargantua →

A LoRA fine-tune on a CogVideoX-Fun video model for object removal. Inspired by Netflix VOID; without public training data, we built a VOID-Quadmask dataset from counterfactual scenes in Unity 6 HDRP with deterministic physics and quadmask conditioning.

The goal is coherent single-pass removal of objects together with shadows and contact effects; temporally we measured roughly 9.2% lower flicker than the reference VOID baseline. Technical write-up and citation live on the model card and arXiv.

Video LoRA · PEFT Diffusers Data synthesis

P/05

LayerLens →

A research line that automatically picks layer-wise PEFT methods (LoRA, adapters, prefix tuning, etc.) and rank settings to cut fine-tuning cost for large language and vision models. Gradients and approximate cost rely on heavy linear algebra.

Hot loops were rewritten in Cython with memory views; on the gradient path we measured about 124× speedup versus pure Python (VRAM and FLOPs awareness shaped the design).

PEFT Cython Optimization MLOps
Research
